Volunteer Management System

Uniting Hands for a Stronger Community

The Volunteer Management System for the Municipality of Oas, Albay, is a user-friendly web platform designed to mobilize volunteers for events, disaster responses, and community activities.

Key features include:

  • Efficient gathering of information and data among volunteers and community leaders
  • Online platform for volunteer registration, tracking contributions, inputting skills and availability
  • Providing volunteers with information about community projects and events
  • Increasing volunteer engagement and coordination
  • Enhancing transparency in tracking volunteer actions
  • Establishing a culture of accountability and acknowledgment within the community
  • Offering training and webinars to improve volunteer skills and contribute successfully to community development
